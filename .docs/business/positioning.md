## Target Audience

```
Primary     : Families with children (35-50yo, 2-3 family units travelling together)
Secondary   : Adult friend groups / milestone celebrations (30-45yo)
Budget      : Mid-premium -- guests who compare private villas, not hotels
Behaviour   : Research-heavy before booking, value transparency over aesthetics
Trigger     : Milestone events (birthdays, reunions, anniversaries, family holidays)
```

## Competitive Positioning

```
Direct competitors  : Private 5-bedroom villas in Seminyak / Kerobokan Oberoi corridor
                      targeting 8-12 guests at similar price tier
Platform presence   : Villa Finder (primary), Airbnb (secondary), direct website
NOT competing with  : Airbnb Luxe / Plum Guide (aggregators, not villas)
                      Hotels and resorts (different product category)
                      Budget villas under 4 bedrooms
```

### Positioning Statement

Casa Hannah is Seminyak's most complete private villa for groups of 10 --
combining resort-scale space (800m², 20-metre pool, dedicated media room)
with a walkable Oberoi district location, 267 metres from Boy N Cow
and 301 metres from Mama San on Jalan Oberoi's Eat Street.

## Critical Flags for Implementation

```
FLAG 1  Child safety disclosure required
        Pool borders living area; some stairs lack railings
        Solution: proactively communicate pool fence + babysitter availability
        Do NOT omit -- transparency builds trust, suppression creates liability

FLAG 2  "Contact us" pricing creates booking friction
        Airport transfer, meals, laundry all listed without rates
        Recommendation: provide range pricing or "from USD X" on services page

FLAG 3  Security deposit gap vs Casa Luca
        Casa Hannah USD 500 vs Casa Luca USD 200
        Must be contextualised on rates page: "fully refundable upon departure"

FLAG 4  Review volume is low
        High rating but insufficient volume for credibility at scale
        Review acquisition must be treated as operational priority from day one

FLAG 5  Old Villa Finder description uses forbidden vocabulary
        "magical", "glamorous", "spoiled" currently live on listing page
        Replace immediately -- damages SEO authority and brand consistency
```

## AI Search Rules

Content should answer:

- Where is the villa located?
- How many guests can stay?
- How many bedrooms are available?
- What amenities are included?
- What attractions are nearby?
- Is the villa suitable for families?
- Is the villa suitable for groups?

Always include:

- Numbers
- Distances
- Named locations
- Named attractions
- Specific amenities

Preferred:

"Located in Seminyak near Jalan Oberoi, the villa accommodates up to 10 guests across 5 bedrooms."

Avoid:

"An unforgettable luxury paradise in Bali."
